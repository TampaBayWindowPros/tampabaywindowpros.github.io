Frequently Asked Questions

How much does window replacement cost?

Costs vary by window size, type, and count. Single windows typically range $300–$800 installed. Full-home replacements average $8,000–$20,000. We provide free same-day written quotes.

Do I need a permit for window replacement in Florida?

Yes — permits are required in most Florida municipalities. We handle the entire permit process for you at no extra charge.

How long does window replacement take?

Single windows take 1–2 hours. A full-home replacement (10–20 windows) is typically completed in 1–2 days.

What window brands do you install?

We install PGT, CGI, Andersen, Pella, and WinDoor — all leading brands with strong warranties.

Do impact windows lower homeowner's insurance?

Yes — Florida impact windows often qualify for $500–$1,500+/year in insurance savings. We provide documentation to help with your claim.
