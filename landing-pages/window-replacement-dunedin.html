Frequently Asked Questions

How much does window replacement cost in Dunedin?

Costs vary based on window size, type, and number of windows. Single windows typically range from $300–$800 installed. Full-home replacements average $8,000–$20,000 depending on home size. We provide free, no-obligation quotes so you know the exact price before committing.

Do I need a permit for window replacement in Florida?

In most Florida municipalities, yes — a permit is required for window replacement, especially impact windows. We handle the permit process for you from start to finish, so you don't have to worry about it.

How long does window replacement take?

Most single-window jobs take 1–2 hours. A full-home replacement (10–20 windows) is typically completed in 1–2 days. We work efficiently and clean up completely when we're done.

What window brands do you install?

We install leading brands including PGT, CGI, Andersen, Pella, and WinDoor. We'll recommend the best brand for your budget and home during your free consultation.

Do impact windows lower homeowner's insurance?

Yes — in Florida, hurricane-impact windows often qualify for significant homeowner's insurance discounts. Many homeowners save $500–$1,500/year or more. We can provide documentation to help with your insurance claim.
